About Me!

I'm very glad you stopped by! My name is María José but go by Cote.

Photography has been something I have really enjoyed since before I can remember. When I was in my country, Chile, I took a photography course in 2017 and after that course I began studying however I could. I also enjoy landscape and light painting photography so much but just as a hobby.

My goal is create with you and help you, especially women to feel confident, empowered and to be able to be yourself in front of the camera.

Besided doing photography I love spending time with my family. I'm married to my lovely sweet husband and we have our sweet baby girl

I enjoy getting to know different love stories and you literally don't need any excuse to capture a time of your life with the ones you love. So let's get in touch!
