My Photography Business: Site Plan Cote Capture Photography

Cote Capture Photography

Name: Cote Capture Photography

This name reflects the mission of my photography business which is capturing memorable and meaningful moments for individuals, families but also capturing it on my way with a unique touch of cozy, fun and airy editing.

Optional Domain: cotecapturephotography.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase photography services, provide a portfolio for clients to review, and offer contact information for booking sessions. The site will also include a way to get in touch with my to get quote for any service.

Scenarios

  • What's her editing style?
  • I don't know anything about her. Where is she from? How did she start on photography
  • How can I ask her questions?

Color Schema

The selected colors are:

  • #E43D12 (bright reddish-orange): Used for headings and footer background.
  • #D6536D (reddish-pink color): Used for accents, buttons, and links.
  • #EBE9E1 (light grayish-beige color): Used for the background and body text.
  • #FFA2B6 ; #EFB11D (pastel pink and a golden yellow): Plus these two extras.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings.
  • Roboto: Used for body text.
